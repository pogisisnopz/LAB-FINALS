World Introduction

CIRCIVANA

An unsuccessful radianite experiment caused a top-secret research facility to split apart. You have to decide whether to confront the enemy on their home ground or to batten down the hatches and withstand the onslaught, with defense options as split as the terrain.

Gameplay Introduction

COL or CALL OF THE LEGENDS is a team-based first-person tactical hero shooter set in the near future. Players play as one of a set of Agents, characters based on several countries and cultures around the world.

COL takes inspiration from the Counter-Strike, CODM, & Valorant, borrowing several mechanics such as the buy menu, spray patterns, and inaccuracy while moving, and slide cancel technique.
